# App UI Design Guidance

These rules apply to the signed-in product UI under `app/(app)` and the reusable components that support it.

## Typography

- Do not use bold or semibold weights in product UI.
- Use regular weight text, spacing, contrast, and size to create hierarchy.
- Reserve display typefaces for titles, but keep their weight normal.

## Shape

- Keep corners restrained.
- Use tight radii for controls and pills.
- Use slightly larger radii only for major panels and dialogs.
- Avoid full-pill buttons and overly rounded cards.

## Buttons

- Primary actions should feel calm and precise, not loud or promotional.
- Prefer flat, dark primary buttons with tight radii over saturated, playful fills.
- Secondary actions should look quiet and structural.

## Workspace Pages

- Dashboard, Signals, and Telegram pages should stay sparse.
- Show status and one clear next action.
- Do not render onboarding walkthroughs, template showcases, or explanatory marketing blocks on workspace pages.
- Keep setup detail inside the actual builder flow or behind an on-demand help surface such as a dialog.
